    • Research

    I‘m interested in bacterial motility and chemotaxis, including how do bacteria sense extracellular signals, what ligand can be sensed, identifying novel chemotaxis proteins, and how do flagella sense mechanical signals. I’m also curious about biofilm. Biofilms are the major habitats of sessile bacteria on the surface of the earth. I’m interested in how does bacterium form and regulate biofilm, what’s the function of chemotaxis on the attachment and detachment processes of biofilm, and how does bacterium balance its planktonic (motile/chemotaxis) and sessile (biofilm) lifestyles
